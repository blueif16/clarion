# Northwind Utilities — Billing Policy (demo KB)

## Late fees
Northwind Utilities charges a flat $15 late fee when a residential bill is not
paid in full by the due date printed on the statement. The late fee is assessed
once per billing cycle and appears on the following month's statement.

## AutoPay
Customers enrolled in AutoPay never incur a late fee. The balance is drawn
automatically from the payment method on file on the statement due date. You can
enroll in AutoPay from the "Billing" tab of your online account; enrollment takes
effect on the next billing cycle.

## Disconnection grace period
Service is not disconnected for non-payment until a bill is at least 30 days past
due, and only after a written disconnection notice has been mailed. Customers on a
payment plan are not subject to disconnection while the plan is in good standing.

## Paperless billing discount
Enrolling in paperless billing applies a recurring $2.00 monthly credit to the
account. The credit is removed if the account reverts to mailed paper statements.
